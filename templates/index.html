Glyrics (WIP)

Glyrics was made to remove the need to search for lyrics, currently it only supports Spotify. The way it works: you log your spotify account (external url to https://accounts.spotify.com) start playing any song, and it will automatically fetch the lyrics and the URL to the lyrics on Genius.com

To start using it, click the button bellow and connect your Spotify Account.



Disclaimer: this app does not have access to your Spotify credentials.
